THE NITTY GRITTY

With TBN you set the terms of your incentive including what is offered, how long it’s offered, and which days it can be redeemed. Customers must download the TBN iPhone app and log in using their Facebook account in order to use the incentive.

This allows us to track a customer as they arrive at your business, thereby guaranteeing a customer has been delivered via TBN. It’s only when a customer actually arrives at your business are you charged. If no one shows up, you don’t owe us a thing.
